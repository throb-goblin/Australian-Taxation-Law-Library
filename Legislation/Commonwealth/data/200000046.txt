1 Short title
This Act may be cited as the Income Tax (Deferred Interest Securities) (Tax File Number Withholding Tax) Act 1991 .

2 Commencement
This Act commences, or is taken to have commenced, as the case requires, on the commencement of section 221YHZR of the Income Tax Assessment Act 1936 .

3 Act to bind Crown
This Act binds the Crown in the right of each of the States, of the Australian Capital Territory and of the Northern Territory.
