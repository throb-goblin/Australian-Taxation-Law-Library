Taxation (Interest on Non-resident Trust Distributions) Act 1990 No. 124 of 1990 An Act to impose an interest charge in respect of distributions from certain trust estates [ Assented to 28 December 1990 ] The Parliament of Australia enacts: 1 Short title This Act may be cited as the Taxation (Interest on Non-resident Trust Distributions) Act 1990 . 2 Commencement This Act commences on the day on which it receives the Royal Assent. 3 Imposition of interest charge Interest that is payable in accordance with section 102AAM of the Income Tax Assessment Act 1936 is imposed by this Act.
Taxation (Interest on Non-resident Trust Distributions) Act 1990
No. 124 of 1990
An Act to impose an interest charge in respect of distributions from certain trust estates
[ Assented to 28 December 1990 ]
The Parliament of Australia enacts:

1 Short title
This Act may be cited as the Taxation (Interest on Non-resident Trust Distributions) Act 1990 .

2 Commencement
This Act commences on the day on which it receives the Royal Assent.

3 Imposition of interest charge
Interest that is payable in accordance with section 102AAM of the Income Tax Assessment Act 1936 is imposed by this Act.
