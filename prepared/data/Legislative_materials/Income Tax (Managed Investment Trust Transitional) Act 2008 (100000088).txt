Income Tax (Managed Investment Trust Transitional) Act 2008
No. 30, 2008
An Act to impose income tax on amounts attributable to fund payments
derived by foreign residents, and for related purposes
The Parliament of Australia enacts:
Section 1 Short title
            This Act may be cited as the Income Tax (Managed Investment
         Trust Transitional) Act 2008.
Section 2 Commencement
            This Act commences on the day on which it receives the Royal
         Assent.
Section 3 Imposition of tax
            The tax known as income tax, to the extent that that tax is
         payable by an entity in accordance with section 840-805 of the
         Income Tax (Transitional Provisions) Act 1997, is imposed on
         amounts to which that section applies.
Section 4 Rate of tax
            The rate of income tax imposed by this Act is 22.5%.
