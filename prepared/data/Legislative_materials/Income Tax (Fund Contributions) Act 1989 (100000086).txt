INCOME TAX (FUND CONTRIBUTIONS) ACT 1989 No. 99 of 1989 - LONG TITLE
An Act to impose income tax upon so much of the taxable income of certain
funds
                  as is attributable to certain contributions
INCOME TAX (FUND CONTRIBUTIONS) ACT 1989 No. 99 of 1989
Section 1 Short title
                                                      (Assented to 30 June
1989) 1. This Act may be cited as the Income Tax (Fund Contributions) Act
1989.
(Minister's second reading speech made in-
    House of Representatives on 30 November 1988
    Senate on 23 May 1989)
INCOME TAX (FUND CONTRIBUTIONS) ACT 1989 No. 99 of 1989
Section 2 Commencement
  2. This Act commences on the day on which it receives the Royal Assent.
INCOME TAX (FUND CONTRIBUTIONS) ACT 1989 No. 99 of 1989
Section 3 Imposition of income tax
  3. If subsection 5 (4) of the Income Tax Act 1986 has the effect that that
Act does not impose tax in respect of the subject of taxation mentioned in
paragraph 5 (4) (a) of that Act, this Act imposes income tax in respect of
that subject of taxation.
INCOME TAX (FUND CONTRIBUTIONS) ACT 1989 No. 99 of 1989
Section 4 Financial years for which tax payable
  4. The tax imposed by this Act is levied and shall be paid for each of the
financial years for which the tax imposed by the Income Tax Act 1986 is levied
in accordance with section 7 of that Act.
