                                  Other notes
^1M Under the Commonwealth Places (Mirror Taxes Administration) Act 1999 s. 7,
modifications to State taxing laws may be prescribed. Modifications are
prescribed in the Commonwealth Places (Mirror Taxes Administration) Regulations
2007 Pt. 3 Div. 1.
If a modification is to:
&bull;  replace or insert a numbered provision, the new provision is identified by
the superscript 1M appearing after the provision number;
&bull;  amend a numbered provision, the amended provision is identified by the
superscript 1M appearing after the provision number.
^1MC Under the Commonwealth Places (Mirror Taxes) Act 1998 (Commonwealth) s. 8,
modifications to State taxing laws, in their application as Commonwealth laws
in Commonwealth places in Western Australia, may be prescribed. Modifications
are prescribed in the Commonwealth Places (Mirror Taxes) (Modifications of
Applied Laws (WA)) Notice 2007 (Commonwealth) Pt. 3 Div. 1.
If a modification is to:
&bull;  replace or insert a numbered provision, the new provision is identified by
the superscript 1MC appearing after the provision number;
&bull;  amend a numbered provision, the amended provision is identified by the
superscript 1MC appearing after the provision number.
