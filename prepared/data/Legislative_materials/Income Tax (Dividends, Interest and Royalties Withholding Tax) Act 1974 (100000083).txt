An Act to impose Income Tax upon certain Dividends, Interest and Royalties
derived by Non-residents and by certain other Persons
Section 1 Short title [see Note 1]
            This Act may be cited as the Income Tax (Dividends, Interest
         and Royalties Withholding Tax) Act 1974.
Section 2 Commencement [see Note 1]
            This Act shall come into operation on the day on which it
         receives the Royal Assent.
Section 4 Definition
            In this Act, the Assessment Act means the Income Tax Assessment
         Act 1936.
Section 5 Incorporation
            The Assessment Act is incorporated and shall be read as one
         with this Act.
Section 6 Imposition of tax
            The tax known as income tax, to the extent that that tax is
         payable in accordance with sectionÂ 128B of the Assessment Act, is
         imposed on income to which that section applies.
