FRINGE BENEFITS TAX ACT 1986 No. 40 of 1986 - LONG TITLE

An Act to impose a tax in respect of the value of certain fringe benefits
               provided in respect of the employment of employees

FRINGE BENEFITS TAX ACT 1986 No. 40 of 1986
- SECT 1
Short title

                                                      (Assented to 24 June
1986) 
  1. This Act may be cited as the Fringe Benefits Tax Act 1986.
(Minister's second reading speech made in-
    House of Representatives on 2 May 1986
    Senate on 27 May 1986)

FRINGE BENEFITS TAX ACT 1986 No. 40 of 1986
- SECT 2
Commencement

  2. This Act shall come into operation on the day on which the Fringe
Benefits Tax Assessment Act 1986 comes into operation.

FRINGE BENEFITS TAX ACT 1986 No. 40 of 1986
- SECT 3
Incorporation

  3. The Fringe Benefits Tax Assessment Act 1986 is incorporated and shall be
read as one with this Act.

FRINGE BENEFITS TAX ACT 1986 No. 40 of 1986
- SECT 4
Act to bind Crown

  4. This Act binds the Crown in right of each of the States, of the Northern
Territory and of Norfolk Island.

FRINGE BENEFITS TAX ACT 1986 No. 40 of 1986
- SECT 5
Imposition of tax

  5. Tax is imposed in respect of the fringe benefits taxable amount of an
employer of a year of tax.

FRINGE BENEFITS TAX ACT 1986 No. 40 of 1986
- SECT 6
Rates of tax

  6. The rate of tax in respect of the fringe benefits taxable amount of an
employer of a year of tax is-
  (a)  in the case of the year of tax that commences on 1 July 1986-46%; and
  (b)  in the case of the year of tax that commences on 1 April 1987 or a
subsequent year of tax-49%.

FRINGE BENEFITS TAX ACT 1986 No. 40 of 1986
- SECT 7
Severability

  7. It is the intention of the Parliament that if, but for this section,
section 5 of this Act would impose a tax on property of any kind belonging to
a State within the meaning of section 114 of the Constitution, section 5 of
this Act shall have effect as if it did not impose that tax.
